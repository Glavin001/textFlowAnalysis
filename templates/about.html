About

This program can be used to give an idea of how well the content of your document flows.

Uploading a doucment will results in a machine learning technique being evoked to determine how closely each paragraph is related to the next paragraph.

You can then export the colour-coded results as a PDF. The suggestion would be that the better the flow the less the colours should change between paragraphs. Lots of colour change suggests that the paragraphs are not necessarily connected.

You can also investigated how the results were obtained further by clicking on the nodes presented in the SOM nodes panel, which will then show the elements that have been put in the node in the middle panel. Finally, the words extracted by TF-IDF will be presented, as well as the full text that it related to in the two panels on the right side of the page.
